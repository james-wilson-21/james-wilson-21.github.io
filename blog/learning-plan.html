My Learning Plan, Goals and Committments

Case in point my working while studying for foundations, I tend to underestimate my workload... Other than that I have a real knack for digital technology and not too bad at problem solving being able to come to a solution through critical and abstract thought. Also I am dedicated to finishing tasks that I start.

During this course I would like to work on my assertiveness. I really care about other and like to give everyone the benefit of the doubt, at the cost of being walked over somewhat when people can see that I'm willing to do whatever is needed without question.

My Committment:

I will work to the best of my ability to keep to task and maintain communication with my colleagues, facilitators and other representatives while being aware of the needs and safety of others.

I will be aware of when things are getting on top of me and be mindful to take regular breaks and be aware of when things are potentially getting out of hand. In these cases I will talk to my colleagues and explain that I require help or time to step back.

I expect to be able to receive help, where reasonable, as things are explained. I am not the most vocal person, so I may need to be checked in on that things are going okay.

I am committed to dedicating myself to this course and seeing that I take full advantage of the opportunities it is presenting me and will keep a healthy balance of keeping home issues such as communication outside of class hours.
